The Carbon Cycle

Understanding how carbon moves through our Earth

1. What is the Carbon Cycle?

The carbon cycle describes how carbon moves between the air, land, oceans, and living things.

2. Main Steps in the Cycle

  • Photosynthesis: Plants use CO₂ to make food.
  • Respiration: Animals and plants release CO₂.
  • Decomposition: Dead things release carbon as they decay.
  • Combustion: Burning things adds CO₂ to the air.
  • Ocean Absorption: Oceans store a lot of carbon.

3. Why is It Important?

Too much carbon in the air causes climate change. It’s important to keep balance.
